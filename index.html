Case Study

Featured Client

Jewish Learning Venture

How a Philadelphia-area educational nonprofit made its programs easier to discover — and freed up staff time — with a custom AI website assistant.

The Challenge

Jewish Learning Venture offers dozens of educational programs, events, and resources across a complex website. Staff were spending hours each week answering the same visitor questions — "What programs are available for my child?" "How do I register?" "Who should I contact?" — while potential participants were leaving the site without finding what they needed. Every hour spent on routine questions was an hour not spent on programs, donors, or community relationships.

The Solution

I designed and built a custom AI assistant tailored to JLV's mission, tone, and programs. Working closely with leadership and staff, I configured the assistant to guide visitors to the right resources instantly, answer common questions automatically, and route complex inquiries to the right person. The tool was integrated into their WordPress site with a staged rollout — so the team could test, give feedback, and iterate before going fully live.

The Results

  • Visitors find programs and events instantly — without emailing staff
  • Routine inquiries handled automatically, freeing staff for higher-value work
  • Complex inquiries routed to the right person — no more lost messages
  • Interaction data surfaces content gaps and visitor needs for future programming
